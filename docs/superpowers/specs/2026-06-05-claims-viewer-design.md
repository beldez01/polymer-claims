## Aesthetic contract (D2 â€” non-negotiable)

Source of truth: `PolymerGenomicsAPI/viewer/docs/redesign/2026-05-19-viewer-redesign-design.md` + `â€¦/src/config/theme.ts`.

- **Light mode.** Canvas `#F4F4F5`. **No** black background, **no** emissive glow, **no** bloom, **no** box
  shadows, **no** neon. "Color is information density, not decoration."
- **Electric blue `#0F62FE` is the singular structural accent** (reference frame, active selection, key
  data, links). Data-identity colors (teal `#08A097`, amber `#B45309`, violet `#7C3AED`, rose `#BE123C`)
  encode information only.
- **Matte materials.** Nodes use `meshStandardMaterial` with `metalness: 0, roughness: 0.9` and **no
  `emissive`**. Soft scene lighting (ambient + one low-intensity directional), no specular hot-spots.
- **Hairline-precise "line container work."** 1px rules `#D4D4D8`; panels 1px `#E4E4E7`, 2px radius (4px for
  cards), **0px** for hairlines/table cells. Elevation by border weight + background tone, never shadow.
- **Typography:** Inter (`var(--font-inter)`) for UI/display; **JetBrains Mono** (`var(--font-jetbrains-mono)`)
  for all data, coordinates, counts, ids, and `Â§0x` section markers. **`font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums`
  on every numeric readout** (non-negotiable).
- **Section markers:** mono caps, `+0.08em` tracking, `#52525B`, numbered `Â§01 â€” CLAIM UNIVERSE`.
- **Focus:** 2px solid `#0F62FE` outline, 2px offset, no glow.

**The metrological register** (what makes it an *instrument*, not a point cloud): a hairline **reference
frame** (bounding box + ground grid) around the force-directed extent, **axis ticks with numeric extent
labels**, a **calibrated legend/colorbar** (exact status keys + a strength scale), and a persistent **mono
readout** (counts by status, edge totals, `layout_id`, live camera coordinates) in tabular-nums. Selection
reveals a node's **exact `strength` 6-vector** and ids in monospace â€” measured, not glowing.

## Scene (the instrument)

- `<Canvas>` background `#F4F4F5`; `OrbitControls` (fly-through: rotate/pan/zoom, damping on).
- **Reference frame:** a hairline wireframe **bounding box** around the node extent (`#D4D4D8`) + a **ground
  grid** (drei `<Grid>` or custom hairlines, `#E4E4E7`) on the floor plane.
- **Axes with ticks:** X/Y/Z hairline axes (`#A1A1AA`) with tick marks at round intervals and **mono numeric
  extent labels** (tabular-nums) at the ticks â€” the calibrated-scale feel. (Replaces the old PCA gizmo;
  label them by the layout's coordinate extent, with a small `layout_id` caption.)
- Lighting: `ambientLight` (~0.8) + one `directionalLight` (~0.4), matte, no glow.
